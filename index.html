I am a Visiting Scientist at Data61, CSIRO, where I work with Dr David Ahmedt-Aristizabal and Dr Ali Armin in medical imaging with GCN.

Before joining CSIRO, I finished my M.S. degree in Machine Learning and Computer Vision from The Australian National University (ANU), under the supervision of Prof. Stephen Gould and Dr Miaomiao Liu in 2020. I also worked as a summer scholar with Dr Charles Martin in 2019. Before that, I received my B.S. degree in Engineering from Beijing Normal University, Zhuhai Campus, under the supervison of Prof. Bo Lang in 2018.

Research

I have a broad interest in computer graphics and vision. I did several studies in solving classification and segmentation problems, as well as image synthesis. My master's thesis focus on the bi-level optimization problems in deep neural networks. Now I am moving my research to the computer graphics and visual geometric deep learning challenges in 3D shape generation and reconstruction.
Most of my projects are available on my Github.
